    • Our Speciality

    Batagor

    Batagor Bandung is a popular Indonesian street food that originated in the city of Bandung. It consists of fried dumplings filled with a mixture of fish and tofu, served with a flavorful peanut sauce, and is beloved for its crispy texture and rich taste.

    Siomay

    Siomay Bandung is another renowned street food hailing from the city of Bandung in Indonesia. It comprises steamed dumplings filled with a combination of fish, shrimp, and vegetables, served with a delectable peanut sauce and a squeeze of lime. Siomay Bandung is loved for its delightful mix of flavors and is often enjoyed as a light and satisfying snack.
